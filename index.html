Hi! I am a third-year undergraduate student at Southeast University, under the supervision of Prof. Tian He and Prof. Shuai Wang in Southeast University..

My research interests lie at the intersection of Systems, Data Science, and Artificial Intelligence. I am interested in finding meaning patterns from large-scale data based on data-driven modeling, and designing useful prediction algorithms to solve real-world problems in cyber-physical systems (CPS), such as instance delivery service.
